Unordered Lists In an unordered list, the sequence of items is not important.
  • List Item 1
  • List Item 2
    • Sub-list Item 1
    • Sub-list Item 2
      • Sub-sub-list Item 1
  • List Item 1
  • List Item 2
    • Sub-list Item 1
    • Sub-list Item 2
      • Sub-sub-list Item 1

Unordered lists may have sub-lists (unordered or ordered) nested within.
