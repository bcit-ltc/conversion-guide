Line-Matching The matching activity is a simple, on-page self-assessment that requires the user to connect a series of questions to their correct answers. Each question can have one (1-to-1 matching) answer. On a desktop, a user drags a line to connect a question to its correct answer. On mobile interfaces, the user simply clicks to match up questions with their answers.

Match the following academic programs to the appropriate School at BCIT.

/line-matching

The order of the matching terms is automatically randomized.

Text Length

Ideally, text for both questions and answers should be kept to a maximum of 140 characters. Large amount of text will have a reduced font size and may not provide the best user experience for students when using this interaction.

Multimedia

While multimedia elements like images and videos can be accommodated into this interaction, it is not recommended as size restrictions will prevent them from being rendered in their best quality.
